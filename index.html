About me

I am currently a senior researcher at Department of Computer Vision Technology ( VIS), Baidu Inc, leading the R&D of virtual human creation. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Multimedia Lab (MMLab), The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2021, supervised by Prof. Xiaogang Wang and Prof. Ziwei Liu. I received my bachelor's degree in Acoustics from the School of Physics at Nanjing University (NJU) in 2017. My research interests include deep learning and its applications on audio-visual learning and virtual humans. Please drop me an email if you are interested in internship or full-time opportunities.
